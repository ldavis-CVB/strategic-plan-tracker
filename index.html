A Unified Vision for Wilmington & Beaches

A Thriving Year-Round Destination

Drive visitor growth and economic impact by expanding travel demand across all seasons through targeted campaigns, event development, and soft-season programming.

Advocate for infrastructure, workforce, and quality-of-life investments that sustain long-term tourism growth while strengthening community partnerships.

Elevate the Wilmington & Beaches brand by curating distinctive coastal experiences, leveraging cultural assets, and amplifying what makes this destination unique.

Strategic Note

Intentional Holds

These initiatives have been deliberately paused to allow focus and resources to concentrate on higher-priority actions. They remain on the radar for future cycles.

NC Submarine Museum

Monitoring Only

The CVB is monitoring developments around a potential NC Submarine Museum without taking a leadership role. This initiative will be revisited once the concept advances through other channels and clearer partnership opportunities emerge.

Connecting Compatible Assets

Pending Review

A planned initiative to create visitor itineraries linking complementary attractions — beaches, historic sites, arts venues — has been placed on hold pending completion of current Quick Wins and stabilization of the marketing budget.

Downtown Initiatives Collaboration

Participating Only

Rather than leading, the CVB is participating alongside city-led downtown development and revitalization efforts. This preserves bandwidth while ensuring tourism interests are represented at the table.

Health and Wellness Tourism Development

Future Cycle

Health and wellness tourism is a recognized growth segment, but this initiative requires dedicated research and product development resources not currently available. It will be formally reconsidered in the next planning cycle.
